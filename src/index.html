Got allergies? Trust only the experts.

Non-stop sneezing, itchy and watery eyes, wheezing, rashing - sound familiar? These are the common signs of an allergy attack, and about 50 million Americans experience this each year. If you’re one of those people, your worries are over. We’re here

  • We treat patients for environmental and food allergies with both traditional allergy injections and via highly revolutionary sublingual immunotherapy (SLIT)

  • We cater to sinus and nasal problems, caring for patients both medically and surgically, as well as caring for patients with salivary gland issues

  • We also treat allergic asthma as well as all sinus problems including balloon in office sinus surgery and formal endoscopic sinus surgery.

Discover the Wonders of Sublingual Immunotherapy (SLIT)

  • A majority of patients that come to us for allergy problems have now begun to discover the wonders and benefits of Sublingual Immunotherapy, or SLIT for short.

  • SLIT is revolutionary for a number of reasons, as it has an excellent safety profile, high rate of efficacy, and is significantly more convenient to the average patient.

  • The sublingual drops are administered by the patients themselves at the comfort of their own home, eradicating the need for weekly office visits just to receive allergy injections.
